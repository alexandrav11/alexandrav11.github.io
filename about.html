About

As long as I can remember, I have been fascinated by human behaviour and movement. As a child I danced, did gymnastics, played soccer and many other sports. Dance stayed with me.

I studied dance for 10 years, starting with classical ballet at the Royal Balletschool of Antwerp, and then changing to contemporary dance The! Kunsthumaniora of Contemporary in Dance and later on focusing on choreography at ArtEZ School of the Arts in the Netherlands.

In 2021, one year after graduating and starting to work as a dancer and choreographer internationally, I decided to change my career path. I felt lost and was in need to reconnect with myself as a human being.

Since then, I have been working as a freelance dance and movement teacher in Rotterdam. I give classes to children from 2 years old up to people of 100 years old within organizations such as the Stichting Kunstzinnige Vormgeving Rotterdam.

My mission is to reconnect people with themselves, each other and nature through movement and dance.
