Teachings

I have been fascinated by the human body for as long as I can remember. At a young age I spent countless hours hanging upside down, imitating gymnasts on tv, dancing around in the garden and doing yoga with my mother. During the past year, I have started to explore what teaching means to me and how I position myself within this practice. I started teaching Basic Movement Classes at Tree of Life Yoga (BE) in 2020, and have recently started to teach Mindful Movement at Yoga Coolhaven (NL). 

As a teacher, my aim is to remain a student and to encourage this to everyone during my classes. I try to stay curious and open to different opinions and perspectives in the hope that I can continue learning and pass this knowledge on to others.
