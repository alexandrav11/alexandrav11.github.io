69
2017

This project was inspired by Kiss, the work of Tino Sehgal, and the way he precisely choreographed and constructed a private yet public situation. 69 was a secret that no one knew about besides me and a camera person. Two performers rehearsed around the concept of a hug unknowingly that they would be presenting themselves in the central station of Arnhem (NL). This project led to a live performative experiment and a video recording which was shared at ArtEZ University of the Arts as a part of the rework of the first year solos.
